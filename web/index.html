Project Overview:

Some people own many spices which they use while cooking, and organizing these spices may prove to be difficult. This is where our project, the Super Susan, comes in to simplify things. It is a motorized, smart lazy susan which will present the user with whatever spice they select, saving them the hassle of searching for it.


Lazy susans are widespread, as are motorized versions of them; however, no smart lazy susan that can rotate itself in order to present its contents is available on the market.

Project Specific Success Criteria:

  1. PSSC #1: An ability to take input instructions from the user.
  2. PSSC #2: An ability to turn desired object to delivery location.
  3. PSSC #3: An ability to monitor the contents of the Super Susan (both number of items and their associated names).
  4. PSSC #4: An ability to return the remaining amount of contents of each container.
  5. PSSC #5: An ability to view the Super Susan's inventory on a mobile device.
